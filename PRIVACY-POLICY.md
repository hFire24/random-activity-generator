# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 29, 2026  
**Last Updated:** January 29, 2026

## Overview

Random Activity Generator ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and store information when you use our application.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- **Email address and password**: Used for authentication via Firebase Authentication
- **User ID**: Automatically generated by Firebase to identify your account

### Activity Data
- **Activities and categories**: Tasks and categories you create or modify
- **Completed activities**: Records of activities you've marked as complete
- **Secrets array**: Optional private data you choose to store
- **Theme preferences**: Your selected visual theme
- **Current task**: Your active task selection

## How We Store Your Information

### Local Storage
- All activity data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage
- This data remains on your device and is not automatically transmitted to any server
- Local storage is used for offline functionality and quick access

### Firebase Cloud Storage
- When you log in, your data is synced to Firebase Firestore (Google Cloud)
- This allows you to access your activities across multiple devices
- Data is stored securely in Firebase's cloud infrastructure
- Only you can access your data when authenticated

### Offline Mode
- You can use the app without an account
- In offline mode, all data remains only in your browser's localStorage
- No data is transmitted to any server in offline mode

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:
- Authenticate your account
- Store and sync your activities across devices
- Maintain your preferences and settings
- Provide offline functionality

## Data Sharing

We **do not**:
- Share your data with third parties
- Sell your information
- Use your data for advertising
- Track your behavior for analytics
- Use cookies for marketing purposes

## Data Security

- Authentication is handled securely by Firebase Authentication
- All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
- Firebase security rules protect your data from unauthorized access
- Only authenticated users can access their own data

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View all your stored data at any time through the app
- **Modify**: Edit or delete any of your activities or data
- **Export**: Download your data using the Export button in the Manage Activities page
- **Delete**: Delete your account and all associated data directly from the app settings (account deletion is permanent and immediate)

## Data Retention

- Your data is retained as long as your account is active
- If you delete your account, all associated data will be permanently removed
- Local storage data remains until you clear your browser data

## Cookies and Local Storage

The App uses:
- **Essential cookies**: Firebase uses httpOnly cookies for authentication (required for the service to function)
- **localStorage**: Stores your activities and preferences locally in your browser
- **No tracking cookies**: We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking cookies

## Third-Party Services

We use Firebase (Google Cloud Platform) for:
- User authentication
- Cloud data storage (Firestore)

Firebase's privacy practices are governed by [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
