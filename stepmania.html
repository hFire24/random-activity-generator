Play Stepmania

  1. Get your dance pad out.
  2. Change to appropriate clothes.
  3. Launch Stepmania.
  4. Play a Level 4-7 song to warm up.
    • [4] Electricity
    • [5] Revival of Paradise
    • [7] Disco Pop
    • [7] Strictly Business
  5. Choose a path.

Path of Touhou

  1. Play charts you've played starting from April 28, 2012.
  2. Play charts you've played starting from May 29, 2012.
  3. Play courses from Touhou Pad Pack 5 or 7.
    • TPP5: Stage 11 Hard
    • TPP5: Final Rush Medium
    • TPP7: Courses 1A, 5A, 7A, 9A, and AA Hard
    • TPP7: Elite Course Medium

Path of Completion

  1. Play every medium chart from Condor's Overly Cool and Kawaii Simfiles 01-04.
  2. Play every hard chart from Mute Sims 1.

Path of Stamina

  1. Currently, you can handle basic 110BPM stamina charts.
  2. At that stage, master the 90-110BPM range.
  3. Play the 8s and 9s of the Starter Pack of Stamina.

Path of Miscellaneous

  1. Play the CNIT480 course
  2. Keep playing Level 5-8 songs for the first 40 minutes.
  3. By the 40-minute mark, you can play 9s or 10s if you feel like it.
